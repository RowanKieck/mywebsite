Here are afew hidden Gems in South Africa 

Hidden Gems are courses that not many people know about but they could easily be a tour course or a championship course.I have 3 hiddem gem courses located around South Africa 

Olivewood is a hidden course by Cinsta beach and it offers some of the best hole designs in South Africa and this course was designed to play winter conditions so the greens are hard and the course is tough. Katberg is inland and is known for its prestine weather conditions and soft greens and fairways and this is just outside Fort Beaufort.Goosevalley is an amazing course on the outskirts of Plettenberg Bay and it was designed by the famous professional golfer from the 60s, Gary Player. This course is so unique and offers some of the best golf experienes and hole layouts in golf and it is a must on the bucket List
